Introduction

DJ Nova

Hello! I've been in the music industry a few years now. What originally began as a hobby quickly became a passion. I love creating music that puts people in the mood to dance and let go. I've always been inspired by space and the mysteries it holds. Much of my music takes inspiration from the beauty of the galaxy, I want my music to take you on a journey.

Music

I would describe my music as mostly edm. Though I am open to anything I drift towards more electronic style music myself. I incorporate a lot of synths and sounds inspired by the world around me. I have done shows for a variety of audiences with a wide range of music tastes though. I love to do remixes and create new versions of beloved songs. My music is created with the intention of making people have a good time and lose themselves in the music.
